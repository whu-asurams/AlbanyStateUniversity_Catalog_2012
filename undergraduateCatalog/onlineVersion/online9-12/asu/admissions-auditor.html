AUDITORS

Students who desire to register as “auditors” are required to submit the following items:

  • An application for admission with a nonrefundable $20 application fee.
  • An official high school transcript showing date of graduation or the General Education Development (GED) Examination.


“Auditors” must pay the regular fees for enrollment and shall be prohibited from receiving credit at a later time for courses for which they were registered as “auditors.”  Faculty members may attend classes offered by other faculty members of Albany State University without registering as auditors. Auditors do not receive credit, but receive a grade of V (see grading system).
