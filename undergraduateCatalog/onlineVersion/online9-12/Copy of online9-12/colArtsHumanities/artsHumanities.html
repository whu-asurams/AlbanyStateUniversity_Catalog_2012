COLLEGE OF ARTS & HUMANITIES

The College of Arts and Humanities includes the Department of Behavioral Sciences, Department of English, Modern Languages and Mass Communications, Department of Fine Arts, Department of History and Political Science, the unit of MPA Program and the unit of Social Work.


Through the cooperation of several departments, the College of Arts and Humanities also offers training in the area of pre-law.
