HEALTH AND PHYSICAL EDUCATION



The M.Ed. in Health and Physical Education leads to Level 5 certification and prepares students in traditional and innovative methods of teaching physical education and health. Successful new methods of teaching motor skills, conditioning, research, technology and coaching are incorporated into each course within the graduate curriculum.
