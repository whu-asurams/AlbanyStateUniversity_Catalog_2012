Undergraduate Prerequisites
Applicants for the MPA program are expected to have three background courses (or their equivalent) in statistics
or research, American government and computer literacy as follows:
3 hrs - Courses in statistical or research methods for social or behavioral sciences;
3 hrs - American government courses;
3 hrs - Basic computer literacy and word processing courses.
Students who are deficient in these MPA prerequisites are expected to complete undergraduate courses as needed.
They will not be allowed to take regular graduate coursework until all prerequisites have been met.
