Introduction to the principles underlying behavior modification and behavior analysis as they apply to various settings.

Introduction to the application of psychological theory of educational problems, including the methods of learning and
instruction. (This course is a prerequisite for advanced courses in related areas.)

PSYC 5555 - Conditions of Learning ....................................................................................3(3-0)
A
