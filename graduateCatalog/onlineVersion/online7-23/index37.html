ACCOUNTING/FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT CONCEPTS ..............1(1-0) 
An overview course of financial and managerial accounting and financial management.
This is an accelerated
prerequisite MBA course for ACCT
6102 and FINC 6101. Waivers will be granted to students who have completed
ACCT 2101, ACCT 2102,  and FINC 3105 or equivalent courses with grades of “C” or better. PASS/FAIL Grading.
