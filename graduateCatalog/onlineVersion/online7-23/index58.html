Comprehensive Examination Policy
Comprehensive examinations are required of the student who has completed all core course work and has
maintained a cumulative GPA of 3.0 or greater.
The examinations are designed to test the ability of the student to
articulate ideas in a scholarly manner in the core, research and major areas of concentration.
